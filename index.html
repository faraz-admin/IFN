About

Real-World Expertise.
Enterprise-Grade Results.

IFN Global Technology was founded by Faraz Mohammed with a singular mission: to bring enterprise-level EUC and cloud workspace expertise directly to organizations that need it most — without the overhead of a large consultancy.

With over 13 years working inside some of the most demanding MSP and Fortune 500 environments in the country, Faraz has designed, migrated, and managed virtual desktop infrastructure at scale — from 50-seat SMBs to multi-thousand-seat enterprise deployments.

Based in Illinois and serving clients across all 50 states, IFN Global brings a hands-on, outcome-focused approach to every engagement. No fluff. No junior resources learning on your dime. Just proven expertise applied directly to your environment.
